About Edmonton

Edmonton is the capital city of Alberta, with a population of approximately 1 million people. Incorporated in 1904, Edmonton is located in central Alberta and is classified as an urban area.

Key Facts

  1. Population: 1 million
  2. Year Incorporated: 1904
  3. Region: Central Alberta
  4. Classification: Urban
  5. Average Income: Slightly lower than Calgary, higher than the provincial average
