Explore Calgary, Alberta's largest city and the heart of Canada's energy industry.

About Calgary

Calgary is Alberta's largest city, with a population of approximately 1.3 million people. Incorporated in 1894, Calgary is located in southern Alberta and is classified as an urban area.

Key Facts

  1. Population: 1.3 million
  2. Year Incorporated: 1894
  3. Region: Southern Alberta
  4. Classification: Urban
  5. Average Income: Highest among Alberta cities, higher than the provincial average
